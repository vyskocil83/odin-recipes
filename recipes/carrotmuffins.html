Soft & Moist Carrot Muffins

Baked carrot muffins with a square of butter on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups raw carrot shredded
  • 1 apple peeled and shredded
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  1. Preheat the oven to 375F. Grease or line muffin tins.
  2. In a large bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt.
  3. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt in a medium bowl. Whish to combine and set aside.
  4. Whisk sugar, oil and eggs in a separate bowl.
  5. Stir egg mixture into dry ingredients just until combined.
  6. Divide the mixture into prepared muffin tins.
  7. Bake 25-29 minutes or until tops are godlen and a toothpick comes out clean.
